About this site

Welcome to The Avengers, a fan-made platform inspired by the legendary superhero team, The Avengers! This interactive website offers an exciting way for you and your friends to connect, compete, and climb the leaderboard. Users can register with their unique Avenger ID, track their virtual balances, and earn money by playing fun games. Test your skills in Rock-Paper-Scissors to win $100, challenge yourself in the Number Guessing Game to earn $500, and prepare for a mystery game offering a whopping $1000 reward! You can also explore other users' profiles, check their worth, and see where you stand. Dive in and become the ultimate financial hero!

Note: This is a fan project and is not affiliated with Marvel Studios.
